                        <p>Triangle is responsive HTML template dedicated to all companies, institutions that organize and host meetings or just want to show the creative side of their small business. This amazing template has a great looking design, stunning animations, single and multicolor variations. It suitable for showing any kind of creative offer or services on your website.</p>
<h3>Triangle Main Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Two unique <strong>Home Variations</strong> with Multi Color and Solid Color to choose from.</li>
<li><strong>Responsive Design</strong> - all pages are designed and developed to the Bootstrap Framework to display perfectly across all devices and screen sizes. If preferred this can be display easily in template manager.</li>
<li>Based on <strong>Boostrap 3</strong> framework</li>
<li>Clean &amp; Creative design</li>
<li><strong>FontAwesome 4.3</strong> - collection of 510+ scalable vector icons that can instantly be customized</li>
<li><strong>Ajax Contact Form</strong> - Used a real working message form script.</li>
<li><strong>Blog Page Layout</strong> - an ideal place for posting and sharing your blogs with all your own images.</li>
<li><strong>Timeline Layout</strong> - create a graphical representation of a sequence of events, such as the milestones in a project or the main events of a person's life.</li>
<li><strong>Masonry Layout</strong>&nbsp;- grid layout which was works by placing elements in optimal position based on available vertical space, sort of like a mason fitting stones in a wall. Great also for the blog section.</li>
<li><strong>Portfolio layouts</strong> - provides you with a flexible portfolio that helps you display your product or photos in the best way. It includes Ajax sorting tabs feature.</li>
<li><strong>Shortcodes</strong>&nbsp;- big collection of Bootstrap Shortcodes (buttons, features, tabs, accordion, progress bar, Carousel, Google maps, Responsive Vimeo &amp; YouTube video player and much more)</li>
<li><strong>Coming Soon page</strong> with time counter great for Under construction temporary page.</li>
<li><strong>About Us</strong>&nbsp;- two ready layouts.</li>
<li><strong>Contact Us</strong>&nbsp;- two ready layouts.</li>
<li><strong>Pricing Table</strong>&nbsp;- two variants.</li>
</ul>
